Summer Fruit Salad

   What You Will Need

♡ Ingredients ♡

  • Whipped Cream
  • Strawberries
  • green seedless grapes
  • Raspberries
  • Cuties

   What You Will Need

♡ Equipment ♡

  • Knife
  • medium Bowl
  • spoon

   ♡ Instructions ♡

  1. To begin wash all of your fruits before you work with them.
  2. Once the strawberreis are washed, cut them into quarters and place the min the bowl
  3. Wash the raspberries and place them in the bowl
  4. Peel and clean oranges, then placce them in the bowl
  5. Seperate grapes from the stems, wash, then place in the bowl
  6. mix the fruit togther
  7. Place the fruit in a bowl and serve wiht cold whipped cream
