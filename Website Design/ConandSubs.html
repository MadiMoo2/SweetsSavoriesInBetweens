Dry Measurement Conversions

  • 1/8 teaspoon = A Pinch
  • 3 teaspoons = 1 Tablespoon
  • 1/8 Cup = 2 Tablespoons
  • 1/4 Cup = 4 Tablespoons
  • 1/2 Cup = 8 Tablespoons
  • 3/4 Cup = 12 Tabelspoons
  • 1 Cup = 16 Tablespoons

Liquid Measurement Conversions

  • 8 Fluid ounces = 1 Cup
  • Half Pint = 1 Cup = 8 Fuluid Ounces
  • 1 Pint = 2 cups = 16 Fluid Ounces
  • 1 Quart = 2 Pints = 4 Cups
  • 1 Gallon = 4 Quarts = 16 cups

Common Kitchen Substitutions

  • 1 tsp Baking powder = 1/4 tsp baking soda + 1/2 teaspoon cream of tartar
  • 1 tsp Baking Soda = 4 tsp baking powder
  • 1 Cup butter = 1 Cup Margariun OR 1 Cup Shortening + 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 Cup Buttermilk = 1 Cup Yogurt
  • 1 Cup Corn Syrup = 1 1/4 cup white sugar + 1/3 Cup water
