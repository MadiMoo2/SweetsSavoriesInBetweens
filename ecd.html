   What You Will Need

♡ Ingredients ♡

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • ¾ cup packed brown sugar
  • ½ cup butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • ½ cup milk chocolate chips
  • ½ cup mini chocolate chips

   What You Will Need

♡ Equipment ♡

  • Meausring cups
  • Measuring Spoons
  • Big Mixing Bowl
  • Spatula

   ♡ Instructions ♡

  1. To heat-treat your flour so it is safe to use: Place flour in a microwave-safe dish and cook for 1 minute and 15 seconds, stirring it every 15 seconds. Set aside.
  2. Cream together butter and sugars until combined.
  3. Mix in vanilla nad salt
  4. Mix in the flour, at this point your dough should be crumbly
  5. stir in milk until the dough is perfectly combined
  6. Fold in the chocolate chips
  7. Place in fridge until ready to enjoy
