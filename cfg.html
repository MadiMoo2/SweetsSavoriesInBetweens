   What You Will Need

♡ Ingredients ♡

  • 6 oz package jello of your choice
  • Red Seedless grapes

   What You Will Need

♡ Equipment ♡

  • Big Mixing Bowl
  • Spatula

   ♡ Instructions ♡

  1. Seperate your grapes from the stem and wash
  2. Pat the grapes dry with a paper towel
  3. Sprinkle jello pouch over grapes
  4. Mix until all the grapes are coated
  5. cover and place in a freezer
  6. leave in freezer for 1hour 30 minutes then enjoy
