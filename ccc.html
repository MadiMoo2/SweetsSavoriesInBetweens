   What You Will Need

♡ Ingredients ♡

  • 1 cup salted butter softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup light brown sugar packed
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • Â½ teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips (14 oz)

   What You Will Need

♡ Equipment ♡

  • Meausring cups
  • Measuring Spoons
  • Big Mixing Bowl
  • Medium size mixing bowl
  • Spatula
  • baking Sheet
  • parchment Paper

   ♡ Instructions ♡

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F. Line baking sheets with parchment paper and set aside.
  2. In a medium bowl mix flour, baking soda, baking powder and salt. Set aside.
  3. Cream together butter and sugars until combined.
  4. Beat in eggs one at a time and add vanilla.
  5. Mix in the dry ingredients until combined
  6. Add chocolate chips and mix well
  7. Roll 2 Tablespoons of dough into a ball and place them in a 3x4 grid on your baking sheet
  8. Bake in oven for 8-10 minutes, Take them out when they have a golden hue
  9. Let the cookies sit for 5 minutes then move them to a cooling rack
  10. Once Cool Enjoy!
